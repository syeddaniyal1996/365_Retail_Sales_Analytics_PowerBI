# 365_Retail_Sales_Analytics_PowerBI
The report analyzes sales, revenue, profit, quantity, and customer demographics. It highlights performance trends across categories (Clothing, Ornaments, Others), locations (domestic &amp; international), and customer segments (age, gender). It also covers the impact of discounts, shipping charges, and review sentiments on sales and profitability.

# Key Recommendations

Focus on Clothing category → Highest contribution to sales & profit, keep investing in this segment.

Expand international sales → Domestic locations dominate, but some global cities show growth potential.

Optimize discounts → Discounts are higher for younger age groups (18–35). Standardize strategy to avoid profit leakage.

Boost customer engagement via reviews → Positive reviews (60%) strongly align with higher sales.

Control shipping costs → Some regions show very high charges, hurting profit margins.

# Insights

Clothing dominates – contributes the majority of sales ($637K) and profit ($360K), making it the top-performing category.

Positive reviews drive sales – 60% of feedback is positive, and higher-rated products clearly correlate with higher revenue.

Younger buyers lead – age group 26–35 shows the strongest purchasing power, especially males.

Discounts impact profit – higher discounts are concentrated in younger demographics and low-priced products, reducing profitability.

Geographic opportunities – domestic markets are strong (Sydney, New Delhi, Mumbai), but high shipping costs limit international growth.
